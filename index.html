ABOUT AZRAS

Urban Sustainability
Beyond Rebuilding

AZRAS stands for Adaptive Zero-Rebuild Asset System. It integrates long-life RC structures, urban asset continuity, adaptive infrastructure, and sustainable urban operation for the AI era. AZRAS proposes a transition from “cities that repeatedly rebuild” to “cities that evolve while being maintained.”

AZRASとは

建替え依存を超える
都市持続思想

AZRASは、Adaptive Zero-Rebuild Asset Systemの略称です。 AI時代における都市維持、長寿命RC構造、資産継続、 持続可能インフラを統合し、 「壊して建て直す都市」から「維持しながら進化する都市」 への転換を目指します。

VISION

Urban Management Philosophy
for the AI Era

In the era of AI, energy transition, aging infrastructure, and demographic transformation, cities can no longer survive through endless rebuilding. AZRAS proposes a long-term urban philosophy that enables cities to evolve while being maintained, rather than repeatedly destroyed and reconstructed.

VISION

AI時代の
都市運営思想
