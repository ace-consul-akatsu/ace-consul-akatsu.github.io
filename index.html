Vision

Urban Management Philosophy for the AI Era

The AI era will increase the importance of energy, infrastructure resilience, urban continuity, and long-term asset sustainability. AZRAS proposes a way to rethink how cities endure, adapt, and preserve value across generations.

AZRAS

Adaptive Zero-Rebuild Asset System

AZRAS is a concept for long-life urban assets. It connects durable RC systems, adaptive infrastructure, asset continuity, and reduced rebuild dependency into a unified urban sustainability model.

Adaptive

Flexible urban and architectural systems.

Zero-Rebuild

Reducing dependence on repeated demolition and reconstruction.

Asset

Preserving long-term value of buildings and cities.

System

Integrating technology, structure, energy, and urban operation.
